Draft Findings Memorandum (DFM)

This is in accordance with the terms of reference set out in our Letter of Engagement dated 20 August 2021 (“LOE”), wherein Ifinworth Advisors Private Limited (“Ifinworth Advisors” or “We”) has been appointed as a financial advisor by ABC Private Limited (referred to herein as “ABC” or the “Company” or the “Business” or the “Client” or “You”) in relation to carrying out a valuation of itself as on the agreed valuation dates as 19 June 2019 and 15 June 2020 (“Engagement” or “Agreement”) for internal evaluation purposes.

The key terms of the Engagement are summarized as under:

  1. a. Scope and Purpose of Valuation: The Company has issued Employee Stock Options (“ESOP”) to its key employees under its internal Employee Stock Option Scheme 2019 on grant dates as 19 June 2019 and 15 June 2020 (Grant Dates). This would require valuation of the underlying equity and options (“Valuation” or “Valuation Analysis”) as on different Grant Dates for financial reporting purposes. In this context, management of the Company (“Management”) has appointed Ifinworth Advisors for valuation of the equity and options of the Company for financial reporting purposes.

  2. b. Valuation Date: The dates for carrying out the valuation analysis is agreed as at 19 June 2019 and 15 June 2020 (“Valuation Date”). Management has provided the financials as at 30 June 2019 and 30 June 2020 and confirmed and represented there are no significant differences in the financials provided to us for the purposes of the Valuation and the Valuation Date.


  1. c.Non-disclosure of Draft Findings Memorandum: Our Draft Findings Memorandum (“DFM” or the “Report”) is confidential to the Client and will be used by the Client only for internal evaluation purposes only. Our Report is issued by us on the express understanding that it shall not be copied, disclosed or circulated or referred to in correspondence or discussion with any third party or used for any other purposes without prior written consent from Ifinworth Advisors.

  2. d. Scope Limitations: Scope limitations as listed in Appendix 2.

  3. e. Role of Ifinworth Advisors: Pursuant to this Engagement, Ifinworth Advisors did not perform any management functions for You or make any decisions. We make recommendations based on findings from our analysis and You would be free to exercise judgement based on your experience and further reviews (if deem appropriate) for the final decision making and accepting responsibility for the results. We provided an advisory role and the final decision-making on the pricing will be taken by the Client.

  4. f. Sources of information: This Report is based on the information provided to Ifinworth Advisors by the management of the Client (“Management”). In arriving at our conclusions, Ifinworth Advisors applied generally accepted valuation methodologies as on the Valuation Date.

  5. g. Cutoff date for information: This Report is based on information received until 26 August 2021. Kindly note this Report is not updated for any events post this date and any such updates or additional information could change the information herein and have an impact on the Valuation which change could be material.

This Report does not constitute an offer or invitation to any section of the public to subscribe for or purchase any securities in, or the other business or assets or liabilities of the Company or the Target. This letter forms an integral part of the Report.
